This directory contains story illustrations for the Buffopotamus website.

Image naming convention:
- act1-identity-crisis.jpg - Biff standing alone between the buffalo herd and hippopotamus pod
- act2-journey-discovery.jpg - Biff meeting Zeb the zebra in a mixed landscape
- act3-crisis-purpose.jpg - Biff helping animals during the flood crisis
- act4-resolution-acceptance.jpg - Biff leading the diverse community in the borderlands

These images should be higher resolution versions of the images currently in src/assets/.
